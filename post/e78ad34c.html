Problem Description

These days, you can do all sorts of things online. For example, you can use various websites to make virtual friends. For some people, growing their social network (their friends, their friendsâ€™ friends, their friendsâ€™ friendsâ€™ friends, and so on), has become an addictive hobby. Each of the following F lines contains the names of two people who have just become friends, separated by a space. A name is a string of 1 to 20 letters (uppercase or lowercase).
